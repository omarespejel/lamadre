# Lamadre Company Setup & Jurisdiction Analysis (2026)

## Name
Primary: Lamadre (edgy, memorable, from "chinga tu madre" meme culture adapted to "la madre" for branding).
Variants considered: SiktirX, ChingaX (too on-the-nose), Lamadre AG / Lamadre GmbH.

## Switzerland (Zug) - Recommended for Aztec synergy
- Crypto valley ecosystem (Aztec team presence, easy networking/grants).
- GmbH formation: ~CHF 20k capital + notary (~CHF 5-8k) + registration.
- Annual: accounting, audit if > thresholds, taxes ~12-14% effective on profits.
- First year estimate (conservative): CHF 40-80k all-in (setup + 1yr ops + basic legal).
- Banking: good for crypto-friendly (Sygnum, SEBA, or traditional with compliance).
- Privacy: relatively private but KYC for company officers.
- Grant friendly: Swiss entities often eligible for EU-adjacent and crypto programs.

## UAE (DIFC or ADGM free zones)
- 0% corporate tax on qualifying income (free zone).
- Setup cost lower: ~$12-25k first year (license + flexi-desk + visa if needed).
- Faster, English common law.
- Banking more challenging for privacy projects (AML scrutiny on privacy coins).
- Good for holding / ops if team location flexible.
- Less "crypto-native" signal than Zug for Aztec community.

## Cayman / BVI
- Classic offshore. Very private, low cost.
- Tax neutral.
- Harder banking, grant perception issues ("tax haven").
- OK for IP holding but less ideal for active protocol dev grants.
